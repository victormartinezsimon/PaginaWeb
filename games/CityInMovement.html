Proof of concept of the a City In Movement. In this proyect we have a random city with random cars that go from one point to another point of the city. When a car reach the destiny, it ask for a new destiny. The car respect the traffic lights and try to avoid the other cars. I developed everything is in the proyect, the AI, the random city, the traffic rules and the log system.
